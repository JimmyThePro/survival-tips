How to prepare for emergency situations!

- And how to deal with them...

The most common types of emergency situations...

...are those that involve severe weather events, such as a tornado, hurricane or earthquake. Depending on where you live, you might also need to prepare for more common weather emergencies.

If you live in the mountains, for example, you might want to prepare for whiteout winter conditions or avalanches. If you live near a coastal region, then you might want to prepare for floods and other water-related emergencies. As they say, being prepared is half the battle. If you have a cheap survival kit nearby and ready to go in the event of an emergency, it might make all the difference in how long you can survive a completely unforeseen event.

A cheap survival kit should be easy to find, very portable and easy to use. During an emergency, you literally want to be able to “grab and go.” By having a cheap survival kit on hand at all times, you can prevent an emergency from turning into a life-threatening event.
